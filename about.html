About Cookie Cutter Designs

Who We Are

Crafted with care.
Designed for you.

Cookie Cutter Designs was born from a passion for creativity and a love of making things personal. We believe every item you wear, carry, or drink from should tell your story — and we're here to help you tell it beautifully.

Based in Hurricane, Utah, we specialize in custom drinkware, apparel, and gifts. Whether you need one special piece for a birthday or a full set for an event, we pour heart and care into every single order.

We use vinyl cutting, DTF (direct-to-film) printing, and we're currently learning laser engraving — so our capabilities keep growing right along with our excitement for this craft.

What We Do

Our Craft & Methods

We combine multiple techniques to bring your designs to life on virtually any product.

🎨

Vinyl Cutting

Precision-cut vinyl designs applied to tumblers, shirts, bags, and more. Clean lines, vibrant colors, long-lasting results.

👕

DTF Printing

Direct-to-film printing transfers your full-color designs onto fabric. Perfect for detailed artwork, photos, and vibrant colors on apparel.

✨

Laser Engraving

We're learning to use a Laser X tool for precision engraving on wood, acrylic, metal, and more. Coming soon!

❤️

Made to Order

Every single item is made to order — never mass produced. You get something truly one-of-a-kind, made just for you.
